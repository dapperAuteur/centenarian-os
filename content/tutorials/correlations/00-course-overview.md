## What This Course Covers

Correlations and Analytics are the two insight layers of CentenarianOS — they turn your logged data into discoverable patterns. The Correlations page shows scatter plots and AI-generated insights from statistical relationships in your data. The Analytics page shows module-level summaries and pre-computed behavioral correlations across all your modules.

This course covers how to read each page, what the statistics mean, how the AI generates insights, and what to actually do with what you find.

## Prerequisites

These pages require data to be meaningful. You need at least **5–14 days** of logged data across multiple modules before the Correlations page will show anything. The Analytics page requires similar history for confidence scores to be useful.

If you're new to CentenarianOS, log consistently for 2–4 weeks before making these pages a regular review stop.

## Key Takeaways

- Correlations: statistical scatter plots + Gemini-generated language insights (6 predefined metric pairs)
- Analytics: pre-computed behavioral correlations + module-level stats (Planner, Fuel, Engine, Body)
- Both require 14–30+ days of data for reliable insights
- Neither tells you what to do — they surface patterns. You decide what to act on.
