## Narrator Script

Before you can access all lessons in a course, you need to enroll. This lesson covers how enrollment works — for free courses, for paid courses, and for courses that have free preview lessons mixed in with locked content.

---

### Free Courses

If a course has no enrollment fee, clicking **Enroll** on the course detail page immediately adds you to the course — no checkout, no payment. The button changes to **Go to Course** and you can start any lesson right away.

---

### Previewing Before You Enroll

Most paid courses have at least one free preview lesson — usually the welcome lesson or course overview. These are marked with a **Preview** label on the lesson list.

You can click a preview lesson directly from the course detail page without enrolling. The lesson viewer opens normally. When the preview lesson ends, you'll see a prompt to enroll to continue.

---

### Paid Enrollment

For courses with an enrollment fee:

1. Open the course detail page
2. Click **Enroll Now** — the button shows the price (e.g., "Enroll — $49")
3. You're redirected to Stripe Checkout — the same secure payment page used for your CentenarianOS subscription
4. Complete payment with your card
5. After payment, Stripe redirects you back to the course detail page — now fully enrolled

Your enrollment is permanent. Once you pay for a course, you have lifetime access to it — no expiration, no re-enrollment fee.

---

### What Happens After Enrollment

After enrolling (free or paid):
- The course appears on the **Enrolled** tab in the catalog
- The course appears on your **My Courses** dashboard at `/academy/my-courses`
- All lessons in the course are now accessible
- Your progress is tracked from the first lesson you open

---

### Finding Your Enrolled Courses

Two ways to get back to a course you're enrolled in:

1. **Catalog → Enrolled tab** — filter the catalog to just your enrolled courses
2. **My Courses** (`/academy/my-courses`) — a dedicated dashboard showing all your enrolled and completed courses with progress bars

The My Courses lesson covers this in detail.

---

### Subscription vs. Per-Course Enrollment

Some courses are included with your CentenarianOS subscription — these will show as free even if they have a list price. Other courses require a separate per-course enrollment fee regardless of your subscription tier.

The course detail page always shows the price you'll actually pay given your current subscription.

## Key Takeaways

- Free courses: click Enroll → immediate access, no payment
- Preview lessons: accessible on any course without enrolling — look for the "Preview" label
- Paid enrollment: Enroll Now → Stripe Checkout → permanent lifetime access after payment
- After enrollment: course appears in the Enrolled tab and on My Courses (/academy/my-courses)
- Subscription may include some courses at no additional charge — detail page shows your actual price
