## Narrator Script

My Courses is your central view of everything you're working on in the Academy — all enrolled courses in one place, with progress at a glance. This lesson covers how to use it.

---

### Finding My Courses

Navigate to `/academy/my-courses`. You can also reach it from the Academy catalog by clicking the **My Courses** link in the catalog nav.

---

### What My Courses Shows

The page has two sections:

**In Progress**
Courses you're enrolled in with at least one lesson completed but not fully finished. Each card shows:
- Course title and teacher
- Progress bar — visually shows how far through the course you are
- Percentage complete ("12 of 20 lessons complete" or "60%")
- A **Continue** button that links directly to the first incomplete lesson

**Completed**
Courses where every lesson has been marked complete. Each card shows:
- Course title and teacher
- "Completed" badge
- Completion date
- A **Review** button that links to the course's first lesson (for revisiting)

---

### The Progress Bar

The progress bar reflects the ratio of completed lessons to total lessons:

```
progress = (lessons marked complete) / (total lessons in course)
```

Marking a lesson complete is the only action that moves the bar. Opening a lesson without marking it complete doesn't change your progress.

---

### Continuing a Course

Click **Continue** on any In Progress card. The viewer opens directly at the first lesson you haven't completed yet — so you don't have to scroll through the sidebar to find where you left off.

If you want to revisit a specific earlier lesson instead of continuing from where you left off, click the course title to go to the course detail page, then select the lesson from the list.

---

### Reattempting a Completed Course

Completed courses don't disappear or lock you out. Click **Review** to reopen the course from the beginning. All your completion marks are preserved — the sidebar still shows checkmarks. You can re-read any lesson freely.

There's no way to "reset" a course and clear your completion marks. If you want to re-do a course fresh, that's a feature for future development — for now, the content is fully accessible for review regardless of completion status.

---

### Unenrolling from a Course

There is currently no self-service unenroll button. If you need to remove a course from your My Courses list, contact support. Paid enrollments are non-refundable after the first lesson is accessed.

## Key Takeaways

- My Courses at /academy/my-courses — two sections: In Progress and Completed
- Progress bar = lessons marked complete / total lessons — only Mark Complete moves the bar
- Continue button drops you at your first incomplete lesson, not the beginning
- Completed courses stay accessible — click Review to re-read any lesson without clearing progress
- No self-service unenroll — contact support if needed
