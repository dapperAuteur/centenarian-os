## What This Course Covers

The Centenarian Academy is the learning platform inside CentenarianOS. This course walks you through every part of the student experience — from finding and enrolling in courses to navigating lessons, completing assignments, and tracking your progress.

---

## Lessons in This Course

| # | Lesson | What You'll Learn |
|---|--------|-------------------|
| 01 | Welcome to the Academy | The course catalog, browsing by topic, searching, and filtering |
| 02 | Enrolling in a Course | Free previews, paid enrollment, and the checkout flow |
| 03 | Navigating Lessons (Linear Mode) | The lesson viewer, progress tracker, and Previous/Next navigation |
| 04 | CYOA Adventure Mode | Choose Your Own Adventure crossroads — semantic, linear, and random paths |
| 05 | Tracking Your Progress | My Courses dashboard, completion percentages, and re-attempting courses |
| 06 | Assignments | Submitting work, metric slots, and reading teacher feedback |
| 07 | Messaging Your Teacher | The course messaging system and how to get support |
| 08 | Learning Paths | Curated multi-course sequences and how to follow them |

---

## Prerequisites

No prior setup required. You need a CentenarianOS account. Some courses require an active subscription or a one-time enrollment fee.

---

## Key Takeaways

- The Academy has two navigation modes: linear (step-by-step) and CYOA (choose your own path)
- Free preview lessons are available without enrollment on every course
- Progress is tracked automatically as you complete lessons
- Teachers can assign work and respond to submissions with feedback
