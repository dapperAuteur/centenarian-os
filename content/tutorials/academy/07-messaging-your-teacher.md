# Lesson 07: Messaging Your Teacher

**Course:** Navigating the Centenarian Academy
**Module:** Interaction
**Duration:** ~3 min
**Lesson type:** text / video
**is_free_preview:** true

## Narrator Script

Beyond assignments, every course has a messaging system for direct communication with the teacher. This is where you ask questions, share observations, or get clarification on lesson content.

---

### What Course Messaging Is

Course messaging is a per-course inbox — a private conversation thread between you and the teacher of that specific course. It's separate from assignments. Use it for:

- Questions about lesson content ("In lesson 3, you mentioned X — can you clarify what Y means?")
- Practical application questions ("How would I apply this protocol if I travel frequently?")
- General feedback on the course

---

### Accessing Course Messages

You can access your messages from two places:

1. **My Courses → Messages** — your unified inbox showing all conversations across all courses
2. **Course detail page → Messages tab** — opens the thread for that specific course

From the inbox, click any conversation to open the full thread. On mobile, the conversation list hides when you open a thread — tap the back arrow to return.

The first time you open a thread, it's empty — you initiate the conversation.

---

### Sending a Message

In the message composer at the bottom of the Messages tab:

1. Type your message in the text field
2. Click **Send**

Your message appears in the thread immediately. There's no draft mode — messages send when you click Send.

Keep messages specific. Reference the lesson number or topic so the teacher can respond helpfully ("In Lesson 04 on CYOA mode, I was confused about...").

---

### Reading Replies

When the teacher replies, you'll receive a notification (the bell icon in the top nav). Click the notification to go directly to the message thread.

Replies appear chronologically in the thread — newest messages at the bottom. The conversation auto-refreshes every 30 seconds, so new messages appear without needing to reload the page.

### Assignment Feedback Threads

Beyond course messages, each assignment submission has its own feedback thread. After you submit work, you and your teacher can exchange messages directly on that submission. The teacher's messages appear in fuchsia; yours in gray. This thread also auto-refreshes every 30 seconds.

---

### Conversation History

All messages are preserved permanently. You can scroll back to see any previous exchange in the thread. If you had a conversation weeks ago and want to reference the teacher's answer, it's still there.

---

### One Thread Per Course

Each course has a single thread between you and that teacher. If you're enrolled in two courses taught by the same teacher, you have two separate threads — one per course.

---

### Response Time

Teachers set their own availability. Some respond within hours; others check weekly. If a course has a stated response time in the description, that's the teacher's commitment. There's no guaranteed SLA from the platform side.

## Key Takeaways

- Access messages from My Courses → Messages (unified inbox) or the course detail page
- One private thread per course between you and the teacher
- Assignment submissions have their own separate feedback threads
- Messages auto-refresh every 30 seconds — no need to reload
- Type your message and click Send — no draft mode
- All message history is preserved permanently
- Two courses by the same teacher = two separate threads (one per course)
