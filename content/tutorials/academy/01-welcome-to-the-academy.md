## Narrator Script

The Centenarian Academy is where the knowledge layer of CentenarianOS lives. This lesson walks through the course catalog — how it's organized, how to find what you're looking for, and how to tell which courses are available to you.

---

### Navigating to the Academy

From anywhere in your dashboard, click **Academy** in the left navigation sidebar. This brings you to `/academy` — the main course catalog.

---

### The Catalog Layout

The catalog page has three main areas:

**Search bar** — at the top of the catalog. Type any keyword (topic, teacher name, course title) and the list filters in real time.

**Tab filters** — a row of tabs below the search bar:
- **All** — every published course
- **Enrolled** — courses you're currently enrolled in
- **Completed** — courses you've finished
- **Free** — courses with no enrollment fee (or with enough free preview lessons to start immediately)

Switching tabs doesn't clear your search — you can combine a search term with a tab filter.

**Course grid** — cards below the tabs. Each card shows:
- Course thumbnail (or a default gradient)
- Course title and teacher name
- A short description
- Duration indicator (total lessons)
- Price or "Free" badge
- An enrollment status indicator if you're already enrolled (progress percentage or "Completed" badge)

---

### Course Cards: What the Badges Mean

**Free badge** — the course has no enrollment fee. All lessons are accessible without payment.

**Preview badge** — the course has at least one free preview lesson. You can start those lessons without enrolling. Paid lessons are locked until you enroll.

**Enrolled badge with progress %** — you're enrolled and have started the course. The percentage shows how many lessons you've marked complete.

**Completed badge** — you've finished all lessons in the course.

---

### Browsing by Topic

There is no topic taxonomy browser — use the search bar to filter by topic keyword. Searching "sleep" will surface any course with sleep in its title, description, or metadata.

---

### Teacher Profiles

Each course card shows the teacher's name. Clicking the teacher name opens their teacher profile page at `/academy/teachers/[teacherId]`. The profile shows:
- Their bio and credentials
- All courses they've published
- Their teaching focus areas

---

### Before You Enroll

Every course has a detail page at `/academy/[courseId]`. Click any course card to open it. The detail page shows:
- Full course description
- The lesson list (with free preview lessons marked)
- Teacher information
- Enrollment button and price

You don't need to enroll to read the detail page. The next lesson covers enrollment.

## Key Takeaways

- Academy catalog lives at /academy — accessible from the dashboard sidebar
- Search bar + tab filters (All, Enrolled, Completed, Free) let you find courses quickly
- Course cards show title, teacher, price, and enrollment/progress status
- Free preview lessons are accessible without enrolling — the lesson list marks which ones
- Click any course card → course detail page; click teacher name → teacher profile
