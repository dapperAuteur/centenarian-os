## What This Course Covers

The Teaching dashboard is where teacher-role accounts create and manage courses on the Centenarian Academy. This course walks through every part of the teacher workflow — from creating a course and building a curriculum to grading assignments, messaging students, and receiving payouts.

**Who this is for:** Users with the Teacher role on CentenarianOS. If you're interested in teaching, visit the Pricing page and select the Teacher plan.

## Prerequisites

- Teacher role account required
- Stripe Connect onboarding required to receive payments from paid courses
- No courses or content needed to start — this course walks you through creating everything from scratch

---

## Key Takeaways

- The teaching sidebar has 7 sections: Overview, Courses, Students, Messages, Live (coming soon), Promo Codes, Payouts
- Course content is organized as Modules → Lessons
- Both linear and CYOA navigation modes are available
- Paid courses require Stripe Connect to receive your earnings
- Rich content types: chapter markers, transcripts, interactive maps, documents, podcast links, quizzes
- Bulk Course Importer creates full course structure (modules + lessons) from a single CSV
- Google Sheets integration: maintain course content in a living spreadsheet
- Sequential module locking enforces prerequisite completion across modules
