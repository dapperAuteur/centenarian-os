## What This Course Covers

The Engine is CentenarianOS's focus and recovery module. It's where you run timed focus sessions, track how your body feels, log daily reflections, and measure the relationship between your mental output and your physical state over time.

This course covers every feature in the Engine — from running your first focus session to reading your analytics and understanding what patterns emerge.

Come back to any lesson at any time — use the sidebar or click the course title to see all available lessons.

---

## Who This Course Is For

Anyone who wants to use the Engine to track focused work time. You don't need to be doing deep work or following a productivity system — even logging 20-minute focused cooking sessions or creative blocks is useful data.

If you already use Pomodoro or a similar time-boxing method, Lesson 03 is worth reading early. If you care about the data more than the timer, skip to Lesson 08.

## Teacher Checklist
