## Narrator Script

The demo account is the fastest way to see CentenarianOS in action. One click, no signup, full dashboard with realistic pre-loaded data. This lesson walks through what's inside and where to look.

---

### Accessing the Demo

Navigate to `/demo`. The page has one big button: **Login as Demo User**. Click it. You'll be logged into a shared demo account and redirected to the dashboard.

Important notes:
- The demo is a shared account — anyone can access it at the same time
- Data resets daily — anything you add or change will be gone tomorrow
- Do not enter personal information (real finances, health data, etc.)

---

### What's Pre-Loaded

The demo account comes with realistic data across several modules:

**Financial Dashboard**
- 4 accounts: checking account, savings account, and two credit cards
- 50+ transactions spanning the last 90 days
- Budget categories with spending charts showing actual vs. target
- A consulting brand ("Demo Consulting LLC") with income and expense tracking for P&L

**Travel & Vehicles**
- 2 vehicles: a 2020 Honda CR-V (car) and a 2022 Trek Domane SL5 (bike)
- 12 fuel logs with MPG trend data and cost-per-gallon tracking
- A road trip from San Francisco to Las Vegas with distance and cost data
- Bike fitness rides showing savings calculations (bike miles × car cost-per-mile)

**Saved Contacts**
- 8 pre-populated vendors and customers
- Selecting a saved vendor when logging a transaction auto-fills the category — try it to see the autocomplete workflow in action

**Vehicle Maintenance**
- 5 service records: oil changes, tire rotations, inspections
- Linked to the finance module for cost tracking

---

### Where to Start Exploring

If you have limited time, here's a suggested order:

1. **Finance** (`/dashboard/finance`) — see the dashboard summary cards, budget bars, and transaction list
2. **Travel** (`/dashboard/travel`) — check the vehicle list, fuel logs with MPG chart, and the SF-to-Vegas trip
3. **Planner** (`/dashboard/planner`) — look at the daily task view and how tasks connect to the roadmap
4. **Academy** (`/academy`) — browse tutorial courses — all are free with CYOA navigation

If you want to test creating data, try logging a transaction or adding a fuel entry. It will all reset tomorrow.

---

### Academy Tutorials in the Demo

The demo account has full access to all tutorial course series. These cover every module step by step:

- Travel (11 lessons) — vehicles, fuel, trips, maintenance, Garmin imports
- Fuel & Nutrition (13 lessons) — ingredients, meals, NCV framework
- Planner (14 lessons) — roadmaps, goals, milestones, tasks, contacts, activity links
- Engine (10 lessons) — focus timer, pomodoro, debrief, pain log, analytics
- Health Metrics (8 lessons) — daily logs, wearable sync, body composition
- Finance (10 lessons) — accounts, transactions, budgets, contacts, autocomplete
- Coach & Gems (8 lessons) — AI coaching and data consultant (admin only)
- Correlations (7 lessons) — cross-metric analysis and insights
- Academy (14 lessons) — student guide with rich media, quizzes, discussions
- Teaching (15 lessons) — teacher dashboard, content creation, sequential locking
- Settings & Billing (4 lessons) — subscription, wearables, upgrading
- Equipment (8 lessons) — categories, items, valuations, activity links
- Getting Started (this course, 5 lessons) — pricing, demo, dashboard tour

All tutorial lessons are free and use CYOA navigation, so you can jump to any topic.

### From Demo to Your Own Account

When you're ready for your own data, click the pricing link at the bottom of the demo page or navigate to `/pricing`. Create an account, choose a plan, and start fresh with your own data.
