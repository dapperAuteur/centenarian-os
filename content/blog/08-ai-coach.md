# AI Coach: Your Personal Guide Is Always Ready to Help

Everyone could use a good coach. Someone who listens, asks the right questions, and helps you think more clearly. The AI Coach module gives you exactly that — a smart, helpful coach available 24 hours a day.

## What Is It?

The AI Coach is a chat tool. You type a question or share a problem. The coach writes back with helpful ideas, encouragement, or honest feedback. It is powered by AI, which means it can understand what you write and give thoughtful responses.

You can create different **Gem Personas** — these are like different coaches with different personalities or areas of focus. One Gem could be a health coach. Another could be a business coach. You pick which one to talk to based on what you need that day.

Your chat history is saved. So you can always go back and read what you talked about before.

## Why Does It Help You?

- You can ask questions any time — even at midnight when no one else is awake.
- Different personas mean you can get focused help on health, money, planning, and more.
- Talking through a problem often helps you find the answer yourself.
- Your history is saved, so your coach remembers past conversations.
- It never judges you. You can be honest about your struggles.

## How To Use It — Step by Step

1. Go to your dashboard and click **Coach**.
2. Choose a Gem Persona or start with the default coach.
3. Type your question or share what is on your mind.
4. Read the response. Ask follow-up questions if needed.
5. Keep the conversation going as long as you need.
6. Come back later. Your chat history is saved and easy to find.

## A Quick Example

You just had a bad week. You only finished half your tasks. You feel unmotivated. You open the AI Coach and type: "I did not reach my goals this week. I feel like giving up." The coach does not lecture you. It asks a simple question: "What was one thing that worked well this week, even if it was small?" You think about it. You reply. The coach helps you find the bright spot. By the end of the chat, you have a simple plan for the coming week. You feel ready to try again.

## Ready to Try It?

Open the AI Coach right now and type one thing you are working on. Just one sentence. See what it says. Sometimes that is all it takes to get unstuck.
