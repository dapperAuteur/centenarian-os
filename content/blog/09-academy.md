# Centenarian Academy: Learn From People Who Live Well

Learning the right things can change your life. The Centenarian Academy is where you come to learn from teachers who know how to live longer, healthier, and better.

## What Is It?

The Centenarian Academy is an online school inside CentenarianOS. Teachers create courses. You take them. Topics can include nutrition, exercise, mindset, business, longevity science, and much more. Some courses are free. Some cost a one-time fee. Some are part of your membership.

Here is what makes the Academy special:

- **Learning Paths** — Some courses connect together to form a bigger journey. You follow the path from beginner to advanced.
- **CYOA Mode** — Some courses let you choose your own adventure. You pick which lessons to take based on what matters to you most. CYOA stands for "Choose Your Own Adventure."
- **Real Teachers** — These are real people who applied to teach and were approved. They share what they know from real experience.
- **Live Sessions** — Some teachers host live classes where you can watch and learn in real time.

## Why Does It Help You?

- You learn from people with real results, not just theory.
- You can go at your own pace — no due dates, no pressure.
- Choosing your own path means you get what you need, not what someone else decided.
- Some courses connect directly to your goals in the Planner.
- The more you learn, the better your daily decisions become.

## How To Use It — Step by Step

1. Go to your dashboard and click **Academy**.
2. Browse the course catalog. Use filters to find topics you care about.
3. Click on a course to read the description and see the lessons inside.
4. Click **Enroll** to join. If there is a cost, you will complete a quick checkout.
5. Go to **My Courses** to see all the courses you are enrolled in.
6. Click into a course and start the first lesson.
7. Work through lessons at your own pace. Check off each one when done.

## A Quick Example

You want to learn more about sleep and recovery. You search the Academy for "sleep" and find a course called "Sleep Better, Live Longer." It has 8 lessons. You enroll for free. You take one lesson each morning over eight days. Each lesson takes about ten minutes. By the end, you have changed two habits — your phone goes away an hour before bed, and you drink less caffeine after noon. Your sleep score in the Health Metrics module starts to rise. Learning turned into action.
