# Weekly Review: End Every Week Smarter Than You Started It

Most people end their week and just move on to the next one without looking back. But the people who grow the fastest are the ones who stop, look at what happened, and learn from it. The Weekly Review makes that easy.

## What Is It?

The Weekly Review is a tool that pulls together everything you did during the week and helps you make sense of it. It looks at your Planner tasks, your focus sessions from the Engine, and your meals from Fuel. Then it generates a smart summary for you — powered by AI.

Think of it like a report card for your week. But instead of grades, it shows you your wins, your patterns, and your opportunities to grow.

## Why Does It Help You?

- You see your whole week in one place — not just one part of it.
- The AI spots patterns you might miss on your own.
- You end the week feeling clear instead of scattered.
- You walk into the next week with a plan, not just a hope.
- Over months, you can look back and see how much you have grown.

## How To Use It — Step by Step

1. Go to your dashboard and click **Weekly Review**.
2. At the end of your week — Friday or Sunday works well — click **Generate Review**.
3. The AI looks at your data from that week. It takes about 30 seconds.
4. Read your review. It will show you what you accomplished, what patterns showed up, and a suggestion or two for next week.
5. Add your own notes if you want. Anything the AI missed or something personal you want to remember.
6. Save the review. It stays in your history forever.

## A Quick Example

You had a tough week. You feel like you did not do much. You open the Weekly Review and click Generate. The AI tells you: you completed 14 out of 18 tasks, you logged 4 focus sessions for a total of 1 hour and 40 minutes, and you ate well on 5 out of 7 days. You realize the week was not as bad as it felt. The AI also notes that your focus sessions were shorter than usual and suggests trying to do one session before checking messages in the morning. That one tip changes how you work the next week.

## Ready to Try It?

At the end of this week, take 10 minutes for your Weekly Review. It is the best 10 minutes you can spend on your own growth. Your future self will thank you.
