# The Planner: Turn Your Big Dreams Into Daily Wins

You have big goals. Maybe you want to get healthy. Maybe you want to start a business. But big goals can feel scary. The Planner makes them easy.

## What Is It?

The Planner is like a map for your life. It helps you go from a huge dream all the way down to what you should do today. Think of it like a road trip. You pick where you want to go. Then you figure out the roads. Then you pack your bag for today.

The Planner has four levels:

- **Roadmap** — Your big picture. Where do you want to be in 5 years?
- **Goals** — The stops along the way. What do you need to reach that future?
- **Milestones** — Checkpoints. How do you know you are making progress?
- **Tasks** — What you do today. Small, clear actions.

## Why Does It Help You?

- Big goals stop feeling scary when you break them into pieces.
- You always know what to do next. No more guessing.
- You can see how far you have come. That feels great.
- You can plan for the week or the month — not just today.
- It tracks costs too, so you can plan your money along the way.

## How To Use It — Step by Step

1. Go to your dashboard and click **Planner**.
2. Click **New Roadmap**. Give it a name. Example: "Get Healthy by 2027."
3. Add a **Goal** inside that roadmap. Example: "Lose 20 pounds."
4. Add **Milestones** under that goal. Example: "Lose first 5 pounds."
5. Add **Tasks** under each milestone. Example: "Walk 20 minutes today."
6. Check off tasks as you finish them. Watch your progress bar grow.

## A Quick Example

Say you want to run a 5K race. You make a roadmap called "Run My First 5K." Your goal is "Be able to run 30 minutes without stopping." A milestone is "Run 10 minutes without stopping." Your task for today? "Go for a 5-minute jog." That is it. One small step. You can do that.

## Ready to Try It?

Open the Planner and write down one big dream. Then break it into one small task for today. That one step is how every big goal starts.
