- **Authentication:** Supabase Auth with secure session management
- **Field-level encryption:** Planned for sensitive health data fields
- **Access logging:** All data access is logged for audit purposes
- **Differential privacy:** Cohort aggregate data uses minimum cohort sizes to prevent identification

**Active accounts:** Your data is retained as long as your account is active.

**What deletion removes:**
- All daily health metric logs
- All experiment data, adherence logs, and results
- All coaching consents (both as grantor and grantee)
- All profile information
- Community posts attributed to your name (anonymized to "Deleted User")

**What deletion does NOT remove:**
- Anonymized aggregate data already published in Cohort Reports (not individually identifiable)
- Assignment submissions required for institutional records (retained for 3 years per educational record-keeping requirements, then deleted)

**Data export:** You may export all of your personal health data at any time as a CSV file from Settings > Data > Export My Data.

## Document 7: Community Guidelines

### CentenarianOS Community Guidelines

*Displayed in community settings and referenced during onboarding*

---

#### Our Community Purpose

The CentenarianOS community exists to support each other in learning health data skills and building healthier habits. We are students, coaches, and wellness enthusiasts — not medical professionals (unless separately credentialed).

#### Core Rules

**1. No Medical Advice**
Share your personal experience freely ("When I tried X, my data showed Y"). Do not tell others what they should do for medical conditions ("You should take X supplement for your heart rate"). If someone describes symptoms that concern you, encourage them to consult a healthcare provider.

**2. Respect Privacy**
Never share another person's health data, experiment results, or personal information without their explicit consent. Screenshots of coaching views, shared dashboards, or private messages are not permitted in public forums.

**3. Evidence Over Opinion**
When sharing health-related information, distinguish between your personal observations, published research, and general opinion. Cite sources when referencing studies. Label speculation as speculation.

**4. Be Constructive**
Feedback on others' experiments, coaching playbooks, or health plans should be specific, respectful, and aimed at helping them improve. Avoid dismissive, condescending, or judgmental language.

**5. No Commercial Promotion**
Do not promote commercial products, supplements, coaching services, or affiliate links without explicit authorization from CentenarianOS staff. Genuine tool recommendations (e.g., "I found App X helpful for tracking sleep") are welcome.

**6. Scope of Practice**
Community Coaches (graduates of the Data-Informed Coach course) help with data interpretation and accountability. They do not diagnose, prescribe, or treat. If you hold a healthcare license, you may mention your credentials but must clarify when you are speaking as a peer community member vs. in a professional capacity.

#### Enforcement

Violations are handled through progressive steps: private guidance → content removal with explanation → temporary restriction → account review. Serious violations (sharing others' data without consent, providing dangerous health advice) may result in immediate suspension.

#### Reporting

Use the "Report" button on any post or message, or email community@centenarianos.com.
