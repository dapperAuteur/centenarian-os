ADHD Testing & Management
At Rise Wellness, we offer comprehensive ADHD testing and management using a holistic, evidence-based approach. Our care is designed to support focus, organization, emotional regulation, and overall well-being while addressing the whole person.



ADHD services include:

ADHD testing and diagnostic evaluation, offered conveniently and completed from home
Individualized treatment planning
Non-controlled medication options when appropriate
Lifestyle and executive functioning support
Controlled medication management for eligible clients


Clients seeking controlled medications for ADHD are required to complete formal ADHD testing through Rise Wellness or provide documentation of a confirmed ADHD diagnosis from a licensed mental health provider.

Comprehensive Testing Services
At Rise Wellness, we offer advanced testing services to support accurate diagnosis and personalized treatment planning. Our goal is to promote safe, effective care through thoughtful assessment and informed medication management.



Testing services include:



ADHD Testing
Computerized ADHD testing provides a convenient and accurate assessment of symptoms and can be completed from home using a computer with a webcam.



GeneSight® Testing
Pharmacogenomic testing offers insight into how your genetics may influence medication response, helping guide more personalized treatment decisions.



Routine Lab Testing
Laboratory testing is available to monitor medication effectiveness and safety. Labs may be completed locally or through at-home testing kits for added convenience.

Take control of your care with precise, personalized testing. Contact us to learn more.
